About Nisarga Sri Vinayaka Minerals

At Nisarga Sri Vinayaka Minerals, we believe that access to clean, safe, and refreshing drinking water is a fundamental right. Based in Devanahalli, we have been serving the community with premium quality 20L water cans under our flagship brand - NISARGA.

Our state-of-the-art processing facility ensures that every drop of water undergoes rigorous multi-stage purification, including RO, UV, and Ozonation, to meet and exceed ISI standards. Located near Vinayaka Theater in Devanahalli, our plant is a landmark of quality and hygiene in the region.

We take pride in our semi-automated bottling process that minimizes human contact, ensuring that the water you drink is as pure as nature intended. From local households to large corporate offices, Nisarga is the trusted choice for reliable water delivery.
