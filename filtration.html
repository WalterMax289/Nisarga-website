Quality Control

Our 7-Stage Purification Process

01

Multi-Media Filtration

Removes large suspended particles, turbidity, and physical impurities from raw water.

02

Activated Carbon Filtration

Eliminates chlorine, pesticides, odors, and organic chemicals to ensure the best taste.

03

Micron Filtration

Series of filters that catch fine particles as small as 5 microns for absolute clarity.

04

Reverse Osmosis (RO)

Removes dissolved solids (TDS), heavy metals, and viruses using semi-permeable membranes.

05

UV Sterilization

Powerful Ultraviolet rays deactivate bacteria and microbes without adding any chemicals.

06

Ozonation

Highly reactive oxygen (O3) provides a final shield against pathogens and adds extra freshness.

07

ISI Controlled Bottling

Automatic filling and capping in a sterile environment, following strict IS:14543 standards.

Every drop is monitored for quality before reaching your door.
